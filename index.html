Phone Bill Widgets

During the codeX bootcamp, I had to create functions that sum up a phone bill,the widgets below are the front end of the bill calculator.

Calculate bill

Enter a string with 'call' or 'sms' seperated by commas. When the Calculate button is pressed, the total phone bill for the string entered will be displayed.
Calls costs R2.75 and a SMS R0.75

If the total cost exceed R20.00 the total cost will be orange and if over R30 red.

Bill with settings

Input own bill settings in the settings area and click update settings.Once the costs have been set a call or sms can then be selected and added to the bill


If the total cost exceeds the warning level,it will show the total cost in orange, if it exceeds the critical level show it in red and will prevent any new costs from being added. The warning and critical levels are both be configurable.
